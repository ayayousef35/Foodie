Recipe Bolognese Spaghetti

Put a large saucepan on a medium heat and add 1 tbsp olive oil. Add 4 finely chopped bacon rashers and fry for 10 mins until golden and crisp. Reduce the heat and add the 2 onions, 2 carrots, 2 celery sticks, 2 garlic cloves and the leaves from 2-3 sprigs rosemary, all finely chopped,

Prep Time

30 min.

Cook Time

15 min.

Serving

4 servings

Tags: Beef Breakfast Noodles Food

instructions

step 1

Cook spaghetti according to package directions. Drain well.

step 2

Meanwhile, heat olive oil in large skillet. Add beef, onion and Garlic Paste. Cook and stir until beef is browned and onion is softened. Add diced tomatoes and tomato paste. Stir in Basil Paste and oregano. Simmer 30 minutes.

step 3

Serve sauce over hot pasta. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.

ingredients

1 pound spaghetti

1 tablespoon olive oil

1 pound lean ground beef

1 medium onion, diced

1 tablespoon Gourmet Garden™ Garlic Stir-In Paste

1 can (14 1/2 ounces) diced tomatoes

1/4 cup tomato paste

1 tablespoon Gourmet Garden™ Basil Stir-In Past

1 tablespoon McCormick® Oregano Leaves

2 tablespoon Parmesan cheese
